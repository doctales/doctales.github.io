Wiki

The Doctales Wiki is a place to collect DITA knowledge.

DITA aims to be the most important information architecture, but the information about DITA is very fragmented. The Doctales Wiki is made to make it easier for newbies for gettings started with DITA but also helps experts to set-up solutions for more complex requirements.

  • If you are new to DITA, you should have a look at the Getting Started topic.
  • If you already know the DITA basics, you should have a look at the Knowledge Articles.
